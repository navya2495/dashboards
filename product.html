📊 Product Sales Performance Dashboard – 2024

📌 Purpose of the Dashboard

This interactive Power BI dashboard was designed to evaluate and compare Product Sales Performance in the year 2024. It provides a holistic view of key performance indicators like Sales, Gross Profit %, Quantity, and Time-based Comparisons. The objective was to track performance by month, product type, and geography while identifying trends, variances, and areas of concern.

🖼️ Dashboard Preview

🧠 Interactivity and Slicer Usage

The dashboard includes slicers for dynamic analysis. In this case, the user selected:

  • Year: 2024 from the Year dropdown slicer.
  • Values: The toggle allowed switching between Gross Profit and Quantity. The current view uses Sales as the main metric.
This interactivity enables users to explore different metrics across months, countries, product types, and accounts.

📈 Key Metrics & DAX Measures

The dashboard uses custom DAX measures to track and compare:

  • GP% (Gross Profit %): Indicates margin efficiency — currently at 39.15%.
  • PYTD Sales (Previous Year-to-Date): $3.71M
  • YTD Sales (Current Year-to-Date): $3.57M
  • YTD vs PYTD: Shows a decline of -$135.89K, highlighting a drop in revenue compared to last year.

📊 Visual Breakdown and Observations

1. 📉 YTD vs PYTD Column Chart (Month-wise)

This bar chart compares monthly sales performance between YTD and PYTD.

  • February saw a significant increase in sales (approx. $340K).
  • April experienced the sharpest drop (approx. -$150K), indicating a possible operational issue or seasonal drop-off.

2. 📊 Stacked Column + Line Chart by Product Type

This chart shows sales breakdown by Product Type (Indoor, Outdoor, Landscape). Each bar represents sales by type, and the line tracks total S_YTD:

  • Indoor products dominated January and February.
  • Landscape and Outdoor types contributed heavily in March.

3. 🌍 Treemap: Bottom 10 Countries – YTD vs PYTD

This treemap identifies underperforming countries based on the YTD sales drop:

  • Canada: Declined by $73.71K.
  • Croatia: Declined by $48.52K.
  • Germany, Colombia, Hungary, South Korea also experienced significant dips.
These insights help in identifying regions that may need strategic focus, product adjustment, or campaign intervention.

4. 🧮 Scatter Plot: GP% vs S_YTD by Account

The scatter plot visualizes customer accounts based on Gross Profit % (GP%) and Sales (S_YTD). It helps in:

  • Segmenting accounts into high-profit, high-volume and low-profit, low-volume categories.
  • Red dotted quadrant lines guide performance thresholds for deeper segmentation.

🔍 Strategic Insights and Business Value

This dashboard not only delivers sales tracking but also reveals important trends and improvement opportunities:

  • Monitor sales decline early using YTD vs PYTD variance.
  • Adjust marketing efforts for low-performing countries.
  • Identify high-potential customer segments via scatter plot analysis.
  • Evaluate product category performance across time for inventory planning.
The visual and interactive nature of the report helps stakeholders make quick, informed decisions.

🚀 Next Steps and Enhancements

The dashboard can be enhanced further with:

  • Forecasting future sales using Power BI’s built-in analytics features.
  • Drill-through reports to explore product-level or customer-level detail.
  • Incorporating operational data to correlate marketing campaigns and sales impact.
